# Sales Performance & Profitability Dashboard

## Overview

An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze sales performance and profitability across different regions, products, and sales channels.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor KPIs, identify profitable products, compare regional performance, and evaluate sales trends over time.

---

## Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance
- Compare regional sales and profitability
- Identify top-performing products
- Detect underperforming products
- Analyze revenue and profit trends
- Support business decision-making

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Visualization

## Key Business Insights

• Europe generated the highest profit.

• Household products contributed the highest revenue.

• The overall profit margin remained around 30%.

• Offline and online channels showed different revenue contributions.
